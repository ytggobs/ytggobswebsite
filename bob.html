BoB not bob

Steps to install the mod

Download the latest Psych Engine version.

Extract "PsychEngine" on your Desktop then open it.

Click on the download button and put "BoB not bob" into "mods" in the PsychEngine folder.

Open "PsychEngine.exe" then go to "Mods" and enable the mod [it will restart the game].
